Care Plan and Goals

Personalized care planning and goal tracking for Alzheimer's patients.

My dad is 72 and has early-stage Alzheimer's. We've established a daily routine to keep him active and engaged, including morning walks, light stretching, and memory games like puzzles and card play. He enjoys video calls with family and friends weekly. I involve him in cooking and ensure he has a brain-healthy diet. We wind down with calming activities like reading and meditation, and I monitor his mood and medication diligently.
