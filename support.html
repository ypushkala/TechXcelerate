Welcome to the Alzheimer's Support Group

A community for those affected by Alzheimer's and those who have recovered or are in recovery.

Our Community

This group is here to provide a safe space where you can connect, share your experiences, and find strength. Whether you are caring for someone with Alzheimer's, have been affected by it personally, or are recovering from it, we are here for you.

How We Support You

Virtual Meetings

Weekly online support sessions where members can share experiences and connect with others.

Regular Check-ins

One-on-one support through our buddy system and regular wellness check-ins.

Resources

Access to educational materials, coping strategies, and professional guidance.
