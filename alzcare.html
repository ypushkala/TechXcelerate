About Us


Alzheimer's disease and dementia significantly affect a person's memory, thinking, and daily life. As these conditions progress, patients often experience confusion and difficulty with everyday tasks, while caregivers face emotional, physical, and financial challenges in providing support. Despite the growing number of affected individuals, early detection and comprehensive care remain a challenge. Our platform is designed to help by offering early detection tools, emotional support, and resources for both patients and caregivers. With features like cognitive games, health monitoring, and support groups, we aim to improve the quality of life for those impacted by Alzheimer's.
